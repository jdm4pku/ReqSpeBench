Each car part has a unique serial number, and the car itself is composed of multiple parts, including the engine, transmission, etc. In the system, the total mass of the car is calculated by summing up the mass of all parts, where the mass of the car body is 1000 kilograms, the mass of the engine is 100 kilograms, and the mass of the transmission is 50 kilograms. Ultimately, the total mass of the car is 1150 kilograms, ensuring that the mass information of each part can be effectively summarized and provide accurate total mass data.
